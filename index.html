About Camp Meeting 2025

Our annual Camp Meeting is a sacred time of spiritual refreshing and renewal. For one transformative week, we gather as a community of believers to experience God's presence through powerful preaching, uplifting music, and heartfelt fellowship.

Our Mission

To create an atmosphere where hearts are drawn closer to God, families are strengthened, and lives are transformed through the power of His Word and the fellowship of believers.

What to Expect

  • • Inspiring sermons from renowned speakers
  • • Uplifting worship and music
  • • Prayer sessions and testimony meetings
  • • Fellowship meals and activities
  • • Youth and children's programs
